we are Ω Ads

Ω Ads is the fusion of some truly brilliant innovators from a diverse range of arts and technologies. We combine our talents to produce jaw-dropping and never-before-seen holographic, interactive and other visual experiences.​We connect dots, harmonising different technologies into one joined up solution leading to amazing in person experiences and then sharing of these experiences via online and social media.

​

We can take every aspect of your project; from original concept development and storyboarding all the way up to the main event. We work not only on the content development side but also have dedicated teams that cater to every aspect of your project such as activation logistics, Installation design, permissions, venue sourcing and security. We are the definition of a 'One Stop Shop' when it comes to experiential event management whether inside, outdoor, public or private.

​

We pride ourselves on developing and innovating. Interactive holograms, holograms that are designed for viral online content, world first holograms at scales never-before-seen - that’s what we live for. We’re technologists, which means that we know that different applications demand different solutions. We’re artists, which means that we care about producing end results that are not only apparently “impossible” but also beautiful. Finally, we're producers. Every senior member of our team has extensive experience bringing large scale high-pressure projects to fruition, and leaving a trail of happy clients in their wake.
